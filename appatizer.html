Bruschetta

Ingredients

  • 4 slices rustic bread
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt C pepper

Steps

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden.
  2. Mix tomatoes, garlic, basil, oil, salt and pepper.
  3. Spoon tomato mix on the toasted bread. Serve immediately.

Garlic Butter Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 300g button mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • Salt, pepper, parsley

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan. Add garlic and cook 30s.
  2. Add mushrooms, cook until soft and browned.
  3. Season and finish with chopped parsley. Serve hot.
