Chocolate Mug Cake

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a mug. Add egg, milk, oil; mix well.
  2. Microwave 90–120 seconds until set. Let cool slightly and eat.

No-Bake Cheesecake Jar

Ingredients

  • 100g crushed biscuits
  • 50g melted butter
  • 150g cream cheese
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • Fruit jam or fresh fruit

Steps

  1. Mix biscuits and butter. Press into jars.
  2. Whisk cream cheese, sugar and lemon. Spoon onto biscuit base.
  3. Top with jam or fruit. Chill 1–2 hours before serving.
